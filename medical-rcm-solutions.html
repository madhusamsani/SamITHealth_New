Provider Solutions

Medical Revenue Cycle Management — Faster payments, lower denials

We combine process, people, and technology to shorten A/R, reduce denials, and increase net collections. Our approach is measurable and auditable — executive KPIs, automation for repeatable workflows, and clinician-friendly documentation programs.

Eligibility & Benefit Verification

Automated front-end checks and payer rules to reduce claim rejections and minimize patient balance surprises.

Charge Capture & Coding Accuracy

Certified coders, smart charge reconciliation, and periodic audits to maximize correct reimbursement.

Claims Submission & Follow-up

Automated submission pipelines, prioritized AR workflows and payer-specific remediation to speed adjudication.

Denial Management & Appeals

Root-cause analytics, appeals playbooks and targeted process changes that prevent recurrence and recover revenue.

Our Methodology

We implement a three-phase program: Assess, Remediate, and Sustain. Assessment uses transaction sampling and claims stratification; remediation focuses on targeted clean-up (denial queues, coding corrections, AR segmentation); sustainment relies on automation, SLAs and monthly governance with your finance and clinical teams.

Technology & Integrations

Our stack integrates with major EHR/PM systems (Epic, Cerner, Athena) and analytics platforms. We deploy rule-based scrubbing, machine-assisted coding suggestions, and dashboards for finance and clinical leaders.
