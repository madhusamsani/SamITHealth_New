Why behavioral health billing is different

Billing for behavioral health services requires a combination of clinical acumen, precise coding, and strict privacy safeguards. Providers face complex authorization workflows, payer-specific rules, and documentation requirements that differ from other specialties.

Core services

  • Intake and eligibility verification
  • Authorizations and referral management
  • Charge capture and claim submission
  • Denial prevention and appeals
  • Clinical documentation review (CDI)
  • KPI dashboards and analytics

How we deliver measurable value

We combine certified coders, automated scrubbing rules, and payer-specific workflows to minimize rework and accelerate reimbursement. Our engagement models—project, dedicated staff augmentation, or managed services—integrate with your EHR and practice-management systems.
