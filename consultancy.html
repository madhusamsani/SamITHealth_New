Case Studies

1) Regional Health System — Revenue Recovery

Problem: High denial rates and slow AR turnover.

Approach: Implemented claims scrubbing, denial triage workflows and analytics-driven prioritization. Deployed dashboards for denial reasons and coder performance.

Outcome: 18% reduction in denials within 4 months and a 22% increase in cash collections. Delivered a playbook and runbooks to sustain improvements.

2) Diagnostic Startup — Rapid Launch

Problem: Needed LIMS integration, regulatory guidance and billing readiness for launch.

Approach: Delivered LIMS selection, integration playbook, and set up EDI pipelines to payers including testing and certification.

Outcome: Product launch in 10 weeks and first-bill acceptance across target payers. Provided documentation for CLIA and state-level compliance.

3) Enterprise IT Transformation — Cloud Migration

Problem: Legacy on-prem systems causing operational downtime and high TCO.

Approach: Designed secure multi-region cloud migration, implemented IaC pipelines (Terraform), and validated compliance controls during cutover.

Outcome: 40% reduction in operational incidents, 30% lower TCO and a documented DR plan with RTO targets.
