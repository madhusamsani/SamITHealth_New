How we deliver — methodology & governance

Our specialty coding engagements combine domain experts, workflow integration, and measurement. Typical engagement steps:

  1. Discovery & chart/sample review — baseline accuracy and risk assessment
  2. Staffing & team alignment — specialty coder pool and clinician review
  3. Workflows & tooling — EHR interfaces, coding worklists, QA dashboards
  4. Quality assurance & training — continuous audit cycles and targeted training
  5. Reporting & handoff — KPI dashboards and executive summaries

Deliverables include weekly QA reports, monthly executive dashboards, denial root-cause analyses, and training packs tailored to specialty areas.
