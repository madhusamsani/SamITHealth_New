Analytical approach (how we measure impact)

We combine claims sampling, payer-stratified denial analysis, and repricing simulations to quantify opportunity. Our baseline measurement includes denial drivers (by payer and code), expected allowed rates using state fee schedules, and projected recovered dollars from targeted appeals. We report results using a dashboard (claims throughput, denial type, recoveries, and AR days) and prioritize remediation by ROI.
