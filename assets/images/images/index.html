Comprehensive Services — Deep Domain Expertise

We deliver a full suite of services that span technology, clinical operations, and payer support. Each offering is backed by documented processes, trained specialists, and engineering controls to ensure predictable outcomes.

Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)

End-to-end revenue lifecycle management with integrated denial prevention, AR workflows and claims optimization.

Long description:

Our RCM offering is designed around measurable KPIs. We map client-specific workflows, integrate with EHRs and clearinghouses, and implement automated edits to reduce avoidable denials. Dedicated teams conduct root-cause analysis on recurring denials and implement corrective action plans. This includes payer-specific rule sets, expert charge capture, ICD-10/CPT coding validations, and a dedicated AR recovery team to accelerate cash flow.

HCC & Risk Adjustment

Abstraction, RAF optimization, RADV readiness and submission support to RAPS/EDPS.

Long description:

We provide proactive chart abstraction and HCC capture programs that combine clinician-led review with risk engine integration. Our mock RADV audits simulate CMS reviews, identifying vulnerabilities so that remediation occurs before penalties. We also support HEDIS/HEDIS-adjacent abstraction to improve star ratings and quality bonus eligibility.

Clinical Documentation Improvement (CDI)

Concurrent and retrospective CDI to reduce audit exposure and improve clinical quality documentation.

Long description:

Our CDI specialists engage clinicians with focused queries, E/M optimization coaching and documentation playbooks. We measure the impact of education programs and track improvements in code capture, audit outcomes, and overall documentation completeness.

How We Deliver — Process & Technology

SamITHealth combines documented SOPs, automated quality checks and scalable delivery models. Below is an extended explanation of our delivery model and why it matters for enterprise healthcare clients.

Assessment & Onboarding

We run a rapid assessment that includes claims sampling, documentation audits, system integration mapping, and KPIs baseline. Once the scope is defined, we co-create SLAs and implement a phased onboarding plan to reduce disruption.

Managed Operations

Our managed operations model assigns named teams with role-based responsibilities (coders, auditors, AR specialists, engineers). We provide a shared services model that scales based on volumes with transparent reporting.

Continuous Improvement

Data-driven analysis and root-cause investigations drive six-sigma-style improvements. We target recurring denials, documentation gaps, and integration errors with a structured remediation plan.
