Behavioral Health Billing

Ensuring Accurate and Timely Reimbursements = Lower Administrative Workload

SamITHealth’s Behavioral Health billing services are designed to address the unique needs and complexities of billing for mental health and substance abuse treatments. We combine clinical understanding, coding expertise, and robust technology to deliver faster reimbursements and cleaner claims.

These billing services are vital for mental health and substance abuse providers aiming to streamline their revenue cycles, improve compliance, and focus on patient care. By leveraging SamITHealth’s specialized expertise, advanced technology, and efficient workflows, healthcare providers can ensure accurate and timely reimbursements while reducing administrative burdens.

Why behavioral health billing is different

Behavioral health billing is a challenging process that often involves complex coding requirements. Mental health claims commonly experience higher denial rates due to documentation issues or improper coding. Regulatory compliance and patient privacy (HIPAA) are key concerns. Our team is experienced in:

  • ICD-10-CM, CPT codes and modifiers specific to psychiatry, therapy, and substance use treatment
  • Managed care and Medicaid nuances for behavioral health services
  • Coordination of benefits, authorizations and encounter data submission
  • Audit readiness and documentation improvement
