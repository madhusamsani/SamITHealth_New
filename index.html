Analytical Insights — Turning Data into Financial Impact

SamITHealth applies an outcomes-first analytics approach that unifies data across EHRs, billing systems, labs, and payer feeds. Our teams build validated data models and apply cohort-level analysis to uncover root causes of revenue leakage, operational bottlenecks, and clinical documentation gaps.

We translate analytics into prioritized interventions using a five-step framework: Data harmonization, anomaly detection, root-cause mapping, targeted remediation, and continuous measurement. This approach produces rapid wins (improved clean claim rates, reduced denials) while enabling strategic, longer-term transformations (DRG optimization, risk-adjustment accuracy, and payer contract performance).

What clients gain

  • Actionable dashboards: Executive and operational dashboards with drill-downs from enterprise KPIs to claim-level detail.
  • Predictive recovery models: Machine learning models that prioritize accounts and appeals with the highest expected ROI.
  • Operational playbooks: Tailored remediation plans that map analytics into people, process, and automation actions.
  • Audit-grade datasets: Data lineage and repeatable ETL processes that stand up to audit and regulatory review.

Our analytics teams co-present findings with coding and clinical SMEs to ensure recommendations are clinically valid and operationally feasible. This tight coupling between analytics and domain experts is what differentiates projects that deliver sustained margin improvement.

Certified Coding & Clinical Expertise (AAPC / AHIMA)

High-quality coding is at the core of revenue integrity. SamITHealth employs a cadre of certified professionals — including CPC (Certified Professional Coder), CRC (Certified Risk Adjustment Coder), and CCS (Certified Coding Specialist) — who bring deep payer knowledge, clinical understanding, and audit experience.

These certifications enable us to support a wide range of C2C (Consultant-to-Consultant) and client engagements that require trusted coding partners, such as:

  • CPC: Expert outpatient and physician coding for compliant CPT/HCPCS and ICD-10 mappings.
  • CRC: Risk adjustment expertise ensuring accurate HCC capture, RAF optimization, and compliant chart reviews.
  • CCS: Inpatient coding and DRG alignment for complex clinical documentation and reimbursement scenarios.

We staff engagements with senior certified coders and clinical auditors who work alongside client teams, contractors, and other consultants in flexible C2C models — ensuring knowledge transfer, quality gates, and audit-ready documentation.
