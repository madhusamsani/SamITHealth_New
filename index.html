Corporate Standards & Organizational Insights

SamITHealth adopts a set of corporate standards that direct how we design, build, and operate solutions for healthcare clients. These standards help ensure patient safety, data protection, regulatory compliance, and measurable business outcomes.

Security & Compliance

We enforce privacy-by-design, encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, regular third-party audits, and maintain alignment with HIPAA, ISO 27001 and SOC2 frameworks.

Data Governance & Privacy

Clear data ownership, classification, lifecycle policies and data minimization are applied. We implement consent-aware data handling and robust de-identification for analytics.

Interoperability & Standards

We follow HL7, FHIR, DICOM and ASTM standards where applicable to ensure systems can securely exchange clinical and administrative data with minimal friction.

Patient Safety & Quality

Clinical safety reviews, change control for clinical workflows, and integration testing with clinician stakeholders reduce risk and preserve patient safety across deployments.

Workforce & Training

Ongoing training, role-specific competency assessments, and compliance certifications ensure our teams meet the domain and regulatory requirements of healthcare clients.

Ethical AI & Analytics

Models are validated for bias, performance and clinical relevance. We apply governance for model updates, explainability and human-in-the-loop reviews where decisions affect care.

Vendor Management & Third-Party Risk

Third-party vendors are assessed for security, privacy and compliance posture. We require contractual SLAs, data handling agreements and periodic reassessments.

Continuous Improvement & Metrics

We define KPIs (uptime, latency, coding accuracy, denial rates) and run quarterly reviews that feed into improvement backlogs and customer-facing reports.
