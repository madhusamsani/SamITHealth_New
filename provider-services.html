Value-Based Care Documentation Services vs. Volume-Based Care Payments

Medicare and insurer payments to healthcare providers are now shifting toward value-based care models, where reimbursement depends on patient outcomes rather than patient volume. SamITHealth helps providers transition smoothly to this paradigm by ensuring that documentation accurately reflects clinical value and performance metrics.

Our documentation experts bridge clinical data with payer compliance requirements.

Transitioning from fee-for-service to value-based payment models.

Clinical Documentation Improvement (CDI) Services

The clinical notes and charts created by healthcare providers form the basis of the medical coding process. Our skilled CDI team reviews this documentation to confirm the medical necessity of procedures, ensuring legitimate reimbursement and compliance with payer policies.

  • Improve reimbursement accuracy and prevent RAC/ZPIC penalties
  • Enhance payer compliance through documentation validation
  • Reduce financial risk from recoupments or sanctions
