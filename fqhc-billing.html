FQHC Billing — Comprehensive Billing Solution for Community Health Centers

SamITHealth's FQHC Billing is a purpose-built, end-to-end revenue cycle management (RCM) platform designed for Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs) and community clinics. It streamlines claim submission, automates payment posting, integrates with EHRs and clearinghouses, and provides executive dashboards to drive faster reimbursements and full regulatory compliance.

Request Demo View Architecture

Executive Summary

SamITHealth FQHC Billing centralizes billing operations for community health centers to improve cash flow, reduce denials, and maintain strict regulatory compliance. Through automation, configurable payor rules, and deep analytics, the solution reduces administrative overhead while increasing collections and transparency for leadership.

Key Capabilities

  • Claims Management: Automated claim creation, batch submission, status tracking, and intelligent scrubbing to minimize rejections.
  • Payment & ERA Posting: Automated 835/ERA processing, remittance reconciliation, and flexible write-off workflows.
  • Eligibility & Benefits: Real-time insurance verification with eligibility rules tailored to FQHC UDS and program requirements.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Pre-built executive dashboards (A/R aging, denial drivers, payer mix) and ad-hoc reporting exports.
  • Integrations: Bi-directional HL7/FHIR connectors, EHR adapters, clearinghouse gateways, and 340B system links.
  • Security & Compliance: HIPAA-safe data handling, audit trails, role-based access control, and configurable data retention.

Business Impact

  • Faster Collections: Reduce days in A/R with automated follow-ups and prioritized denial workflows.
  • Lower Denials: Intelligent claim scrubbing and payer-rule enforcement reduce avoidable denials.
  • Operational Efficiency: Automate repetitive tasks to free staff for higher-value clinical and financial work.
  • Regulatory Readiness: Built-in UDS reporting support and audit-ready documentation for grants and compliance reviews.

Technical Architecture (High Level)

Integration & Security Notes

  • Standard integrations: HL7, FHIR, X12 (837/835), and RESTful APIs for EHR and 3rd-party systems.
  • Secure transport: TLS 1.2+, OAuth2 / JWT for API access, and encrypted-at-rest using AES-256.
  • Identity & access: Role-based access control, SSO (SAML/OIDC), and granular audit logging for HIPAA compliance.

Compliance & Regulatory

Built to support the unique compliance requirements of FQHCs, SamITHealth FQHC Billing includes:

  • HIPAA: Administrative, physical and technical safeguards, Business Associate Agreement (BAA) options, and breach notification support.
  • UDS Support: Pre-built exports and data collection helpers to assist with Uniform Data System reporting.
  • 340B: Integration-ready hooks to 340B contract pharmacy and program management systems to capture billable drugs appropriately.
  • Audit-Ready: Configurable retention and tamper-evident audit trails for claims and payment events.

Implementation Roadmap

  1. Discovery & Requirements (2–4 weeks): Stakeholder workshops, data mapping, sample claim analysis.
  2. Integration & Configuration (4–8 weeks): EHR connectors, payer setups, rule engine configuration.
  3. Pilot & Parallel Run (4 weeks): Small clinic pilot, parallel claim submission and reconciliation.
  4. Go-Live & Stabilization (2 weeks): Full cutover support, hypercare, and knowledge transfer.
  5. Optimization (Ongoing): Denial tuning, analytics configuration, and continuous improvement sprints.
