Comprehensive RCM — long description

SamITHealth delivers an integrated Revenue Cycle Management offering that combines specialized clinical coding, payer-smart claims scrubbing, proactive denials prevention, medical necessity validation, and data-driven AR workflows.

Key ecosystem touchpoints include: patient access and eligibility verification; point-of-care capture and charge entry; ICD/CPT/HCPCS coding and clinical documentation improvement; claims scrubbing and payer rules; denials prevention and appeals; and revenue analytics that connect to financial reconciliation.
